About Me

Born in Omaha, Nebraska to a wonderful mother, Carmen Sirizzotti- I was the last arrival of the three children. Preceded by 2 boys, I gained a competive disposition and inquisitve mind. I always wanted to know how things worked and why they worked. At the tender age of 16, I moved to Georgia

I finished school at 18 with a strong GPA and plans to go to Georgia Tech. Life, as it always seems, had other plans. I excused myself from college to figure out what I really wanted. I took a long time, a really long time, to think about that. Finally, nearly 10 years later, I decided what I wanted to do. Coding and web development excited me in a way I'd never felt before. I looked into courses and found one at Georgia Tech that seems reputable.

I decided to quit my job, and give this course my full attention. I'm excited/petrified for what is ahead. I personally love the adrenaline rush I get from the simplest amount of code I've been able to achieve/create thus far-- I can't imagine how I will feel in 12 weeks.
